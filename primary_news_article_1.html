New Reading Program Launch

Published on: April 2, 2025

Our school is excited to introduce a new reading program designed to improve the literacy skills of our primary school students. The program will feature a selection of books aimed at engaging students and fostering a love for reading. This initiative aims to cultivate a culture of reading and help students develop essential literacy skills that will support their academic success.

Through this program, students will have access to a wide variety of books that span different genres and reading levels. We are committed to ensuring that every student has the opportunity to read and explore books that are both interesting and educational. The program will not only help improve reading skills but also develop a lifelong love of reading.

The official launch of the program will take place on May 1, 2025. Students, parents, and teachers will be invited to participate in the event, which will include a special introduction to the program and a preview of the books available in the library.

Program Objectives

  • Enhance reading comprehension and fluency: By reading regularly, students will improve their ability to understand and process text.
  • Expose students to a wide range of genres: Students will explore different types of literature, from fiction to non-fiction, helping them become well-rounded readers.
  • Foster a love for reading: The program will inspire students to enjoy reading as a fun and educational activity.
  • Develop critical thinking skills: Through book reviews and discussions, students will develop their analytical thinking and communication skills.

How Students Can Participate

To participate in the reading program, students will need to visit the school library and select a book that aligns with their reading level and personal interests. After completing their book, they will be asked to write a brief review, reflecting on what they learned and enjoyed about the book.

In addition to reading, students will also be able to participate in group discussions and activities related to the books they have read, allowing for a collaborative learning experience.

Important Dates

  • Program Start Date: May 1, 2025
  • Book Review Submission Deadline: June 15, 2025

Get Involved

We encourage parents and staff to get involved in the reading program. Volunteers will be needed to assist with organizing reading sessions, helping students with book selections, and facilitating group discussions.
