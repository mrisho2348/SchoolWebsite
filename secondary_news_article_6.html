Upcoming School Trip to Zanzibar

Published on: April 2, 2025

We are excited to announce that the secondary school will be organizing a school trip to Zanzibar next month. This educational trip will provide students with the opportunity to explore the rich history, culture, and natural beauty of the island, offering a hands-on learning experience outside the classroom.

About the Trip

The Zanzibar trip will be a three-day adventure, packed with exciting activities and educational visits. Students will get a chance to visit historic sites, explore local culture, and learn about the unique geography of the island. It will be a great opportunity for personal growth, bonding with classmates, and broadening cultural horizons.

Highlights of the Trip

  • Visit to Stone Town: Explore the UNESCO World Heritage site, Stone Town, known for its historical significance and unique architecture.
  • Spice Tour: Learn about Zanzibar’s spice trade and its importance to the island's economy. Students will also have the chance to taste and smell various spices.
  • Jozani Forest Tour: Experience the unique wildlife of Zanzibar, including the endangered red colobus monkeys in the Jozani Forest.
  • Beach and Water Activities: Spend time on Zanzibar’s beautiful beaches, where students can participate in activities like snorkeling and swimming.

Why Students Should Join

This trip is not just about relaxation; it is a great learning opportunity. Students will gain a deeper understanding of Zanzibar's history and culture, all while experiencing the beauty of the island. The trip is designed to complement the lessons students have learned in class, particularly in subjects like Geography, History, and Environmental Science.

Trip Details

The trip will take place from May 5th to May 7th, 2025. Students are encouraged to sign up by April 15th, 2025 to reserve their spot. The cost for the trip will cover transportation, accommodation, meals, and all activities during the stay.

How to Sign Up

To sign up, students should visit the school office or contact the trip coordinator. A permission slip and a deposit are required to confirm participation. Financial assistance is available for students who need it, so please reach out to the administration if needed.

Important Reminders

  • Students must adhere to the school's code of conduct during the trip.
  • Travel insurance will be provided for all students.
  • A parent/guardian meeting will be held prior to the trip to go over important details and answer any questions.

Looking Forward

We are looking forward to a memorable trip filled with learning, adventure, and fun. We encourage all students to join in this unique experience and make the most of this educational opportunity. It’s sure to be an unforgettable part of your school journey!
