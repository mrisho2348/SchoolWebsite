Primary School Exam Timetable

Published on: April 2, 2025

We are pleased to announce the official timetable for the upcoming primary school exams. This year, we have carefully scheduled all exams to ensure students have adequate time to prepare for each subject. Please take note of the exam dates and make sure to follow the schedule to avoid any confusion.

Exam Schedule

Subject Date Time
Mathematics May 5, 2025 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
English Language May 6, 2025 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Science May 7, 2025 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Social Studies May 8, 2025 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Art & Craft May 9, 2025 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Important Exam Guidelines

  • All students must arrive at the exam venue at least 15 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • Students are required to bring their student ID cards, pens, pencils, and any other necessary stationery.
  • No electronic devices, including mobile phones, are allowed during the exam.
  • Please make sure to check the timetable carefully and confirm the date and time for each subject.
  • If you are unable to attend any exam due to illness or an emergency, please inform the school administration immediately.

Preparation Tips for Students

We encourage all students to begin preparing for their exams well in advance. Here are a few tips to help ensure success:

  • Review class notes and textbooks regularly.
  • Practice sample questions and past exams to improve your exam skills.
  • Stay organized and create a study timetable to manage your time effectively.
  • Get plenty of rest the night before each exam to ensure you are well-rested and focused.

Stay Updated

For any changes to the exam schedule or if you have any questions regarding the exams, please stay tuned to the school's official notice boards or website. You can also contact the school office for further information.
