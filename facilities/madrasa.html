Madrasa & Islamic Manners

Published on: April 2, 2025

At our primary school, we emphasize a holistic approach to education that nurtures both academic excellence and strong moral values. Our Madrasa & Islamic Manners program is designed to instill core Islamic teachings, respect, discipline, and ethical conduct in our students from a young age.

Quranic Education

Children are introduced to Quranic recitation with proper Tajweed, memorization of selected surahs, and understanding key concepts of the Quran that align with their level. Daily sessions are held to promote consistency and spiritual discipline.

Islamic Manners & Etiquette

We guide our students on how to embody the noble character of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). Lessons include daily duas, respectful speech, kindness, hygiene, and honesty. Teachers incorporate Islamic values in all classroom interactions and activities.

Prayer Practice

Students are taught how to perform prayers correctly, including Wudhu (ablution), and are encouraged to participate in group prayer sessions. Special emphasis is placed on Friday sermons (Khutbah) and developing a love for Salah (prayer).

Special Programs

  • Weekly Islamic storytelling sessions
  • Quran recitation and memorization competitions
  • Celebrations of Islamic holidays and events
  • Workshops for parents on nurturing Islamic values at home

Our Goal

We aim to raise children who are not only knowledgeable but also compassionate, respectful, and guided by strong Islamic values. We believe this foundation will help them lead meaningful lives and positively contribute to society.
