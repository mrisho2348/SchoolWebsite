Annual Sports Day 2025

Published on: April 2, 2025

Our Annual Sports Day is fast approaching! This year, the event will be held on May 15th, 2025, and we encourage all students to participate in various athletic events, showcasing their talent and sportsmanship. It's a wonderful opportunity for students to come together, compete, and enjoy friendly rivalry.

The day's events will include a variety of track and field competitions, team sports, and fun activities for all grade levels. From sprint races to relay races and tug-of-war, there will be something for everyone to enjoy. Participation is open to all students, and we highly encourage them to sign up and take part in the excitement!

The event will begin at 9:00 AM and will last throughout the day, with prizes awarded to the top performers in each event. The day will culminate with a closing ceremony where the winners will be recognized and celebrated.

This event is not just about winning but about fostering school spirit and camaraderie. Let's celebrate sportsmanship, teamwork, and friendship!

Schedule of Events

  • 9:00 AM: Opening Ceremony
  • 9:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Track and Field Events
  • 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Lunch Break
  • 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Team Sports (Football, Basketball, and Tug-of-War)
  • 3:30 PM: Closing Ceremony and Prize Distribution

How to Participate

Students interested in participating in the Annual Sports Day should sign up by May 10th, 2025. Sign-up sheets will be available in the school office, or students can also register online through the school's portal.

Parents are welcome to attend and cheer for their children, making the event even more special! We'll also have food and drink stalls available throughout the day, so no one will go hungry.

Let's Make It a Day to Remember!

We look forward to seeing you all on May 15th, 2025 for a day full of fun, energy, and school spirit! It's going to be a memorable event, and we hope everyone can participate.
